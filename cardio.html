What is Cardio Blast?

Cardio Blast is a structured conditioning program focused on improving heart health, stamina, and full-body endurance. It combines dynamic movement, interval training, and sustained effort to maximize calorie burn and performance.

At Connor’s Gym, our cardio classes are built to challenge every fitness level while keeping training engaging, fast-paced, and results-driven.
