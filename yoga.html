What is Yoga Flow?

Yoga Flow is a structured yet calming practice that combines movement, breathing, and mindfulness to improve both physical and mental well-being. It focuses on controlled transitions between poses, helping the body build flexibility, strength, and stability over time.

At Connor’s Gym, our Yoga Flow classes are designed to complement high-intensity training programs by promoting recovery, reducing stress, and improving long-term mobility.

Why Members Love Yoga Flow
