What is Strength Training?

Strength training is a structured method of resistance-based exercise designed to increase muscle mass, improve physical power, and enhance overall body performance. It focuses on progressive overload—gradually increasing resistance to continuously challenge the muscles.

At Connor’s Gym, strength training is treated as a long-term system, not just a workout. Every session is designed to build measurable progress in strength, control, and physique development.

Why Members Choose Strength Training

Muscle Growth

Develop long-term training habits that drive real physical transformation.

Training Focus Areas

Compound Lifts

Squats, deadlifts, bench press, and overhead press for maximum strength development.

Progressive Overload

Structured increases in weight and intensity to ensure continuous muscle growth.

Form & Control

Technique-driven training to reduce injury risk and maximize efficiency.

Accessory Work

Targeted exercises to improve weak points and overall muscular balance.
