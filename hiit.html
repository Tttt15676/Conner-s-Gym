What is HIIT?

HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) is a fast-paced workout style that alternates between short bursts of intense effort and periods of recovery. It is designed to maximize calorie burn, improve cardiovascular fitness, and build functional strength in less time than traditional workouts.

At Connor’s Gym, our HIIT classes are structured to push every participant to their personal edge while still being scalable for beginners and advanced athletes alike.
